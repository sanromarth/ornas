# ORNAS Brand Guidelines

## Brand Philosophy

The ORNAS brand is built around the **"Capture Mark"** (v2). 

The logo represents a single continuous gesture — an open circle that *captures* content, with two bezier flanges that evoke a clipboard clip or capture brackets. The right-side opening creates a "portal" or entry point where content flows in. It is simple, memorable, and geometrically stable.

**Official Tagline:**
> Never Lose a Copy.

*(The tagline should be used on splash screens, about dialogs, the README, and the website. It should NOT be used in the primary application header or toolbar.)*

## Geometric Construction

The Capture Mark is constructed from 3 simple SVG primitives within a 200×200 viewBox:
1. **Open Arc** (270° sweep): A circle with radius 58, centered at (80, 100), open on the right side.
2. **Top Flange**: A pointed bezier flourish at the top-right break, curving outward and back.
3. **Bottom Flange**: A vertical mirror of the top flange, creating perfect symmetry along the horizontal axis.

## Logo Variants

1. **Filled (Digital)**: The primary mark. Thick 24px stroke for the arc, solid filled flanges. Used for all on-screen digital applications (app icons, website, splash screen, UI).
2. **Outline (Physical/Merch)**: The secondary mark. Thin 6px continuous stroke for the arc and flanges with round linecaps. Used exclusively for engraving, embroidery, laser-cut stickers, and single-color print contexts.

## Usage Rules

- **Clear Space**: Always maintain a clear space around the mark equal to 25% of the mark's width.
- **Minimum Size**: The mark is legible down to **16px** (favicon size). At sizes 32px and below, the Outline variant stroke width should increase to 10px for legibility.
- **App Icons**: The mark should be optically centered in macOS squircles, Windows tiles, and Linux circular docks.
- **Monochrome**: When color is not available, the logo may be rendered in solid `#0D0D1A` (black) or solid `#FFFFFF` (white).

## Unacceptable Usage

- Do **NOT** stretch, skew, or distort the mark.
- Do **NOT** change the geometric proportions of the flanges.
- Do **NOT** use colors outside the approved palette.
- Do **NOT** fill the center of the arc.
