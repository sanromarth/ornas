# Support

## Getting Help

- **Bug reports:** [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/sanromarth/ornas/issues)
- **Feature requests:** [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/sanromarth/ornas/issues)
- **Questions & discussion:** [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/sanromarth/ornas/discussions)
- **Contributing:** See [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md)

## Before Opening an Issue

1. Search existing issues to avoid duplicates
2. Include your OS, ORNAS version, and steps to reproduce
3. Attach relevant logs from `~/.local/share/ornas/logs/`
